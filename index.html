What Is Health Insurance?

What Is Health Insurance?Health insurance plan is a product offered by insurance companies that covers all costs related to medical expenses of the insured. This can be bought for an individual or for an entire family. The term of the plan is ideally fixed at one year and in some cases one can buy the plan for a term of two to three years as well. With health insurance plan in place, one gets entitled to adequate medical help in case of an emergency or in a pre-planned medical expense, without worrying about the financial costs.

Hospitalisation Expenses

Health plan takes care of entire hospitalisation expenses subject to maximum sum assured and terms of the policy. Most plans also cover pre and post hospitalisation expenses.

Cashless Facility

There is a cashless facility available under which you can avail the best medical care at any of the network hospitals. This way the insured can immediately get treatment without having to wait for arranging the funds.

Riders Available

Under certain riders associated with health plans, you get the claim amount on a lump sum basis so you can use it not only for medical expenses, but also to take care of household expenses.
